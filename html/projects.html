Rocket League Replay Poster + Pycon AU Talk

Python program that automatically uploads Rocket League professional player replays onto a YouTube channel. This project led to the creation of my own now published lighting fast screen recording library 'pyfastscreencap' on PyPI, and a talk at Pycon AU about the project.


VCE ATAR Calculator

A tool for predicting your ATAR score based on subject results for year 12 Victorian students in Australia. Created to help students calculate their ATAR scores and understand how their results would vary year to year. This calculator is regularly used by students all over Victoria in year 11 and 12 having been used by over 6000 students.

League Of Legends Similarity Of Champions

A data analysis project using Python to determine the similarity between League of Legends champions based on real players masteries on champions found through Riot API.
